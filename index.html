Crystal Collector

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  2. There are four crystals to the left. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  3. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score passes the random number.
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on them.
  5. Each time the game starts or re-starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
